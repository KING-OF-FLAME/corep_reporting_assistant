# COREP demo instructions (prototype test corpus)

Paragraph 1:
C01.00 Own Funds extract includes key own funds components such as CET1, AT1 and Tier 2. Values should be reported consistently in the selected unit and currency.

Paragraph 2:
CET1 after regulatory adjustments is derived by subtracting total CET1 deductions from CET1 before regulatory adjustments.

Paragraph 3:
Tier 1 capital is derived as CET1 after regulatory adjustments plus Additional Tier 1 (AT1).

Paragraph 4:
Total Own Funds is derived as Tier 1 capital plus Tier 2 (T2).

Paragraph 5:
C02.00 ratios may be derived using Total Risk Exposure Amount (TREA). CET1 ratio equals CET1 after regulatory adjustments divided by TREA, expressed as a percent.

Paragraph 6:
Tier 1 ratio equals Tier 1 capital divided by TREA, expressed as a percent. Total capital ratio equals Total Own Funds divided by TREA, expressed as a percent.
